Attending the JDSE

This fifth edition of the Paris-Saclay Junior Conference on Data Science and Engineering (JDSE) is only addressed to students and researchers at the University Paris-Saclay and Institut Polytechnique de Paris. Due to the uncertainty surrounding the sanitary conditions, this conference will be held online, via the Collaborate platform through the browser, not requiring to install any separate software.

Registration will be done via an online form that will be available soon.
